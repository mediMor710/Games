This Repository has couple of simple games that i created so i can train on the logical thinking.

1. Dice Rolling:
It is a game where players roll dice to generate random outcomes that drive gameplay,
such as moving pieces, resolving conflicts, or triggering events.
2. Guess the number:
It is a game where one player thinks of a number within a specified range,
and the other player tries to guess it. After each guess, the player is informed whether their guess is too high, too low, or correct,
allowing them to narrow down their options until they find the right number.
3. Rock Paper Scisors:
Rock paper scissors is a hand game typically played between two players, where each player simultaneously forms one of three shapes:
rock (a closed fist), paper (a flat hand), or scissors (a V shape with two fingers).
The game has three possible outcomes: rock crushes scissors, scissors cuts paper, and paper covers rock, with ties resulting in a replay.
4. Hit Targets:
Hit the Target is a skill-based game where players aim and shoot at moving or static targets to earn points, rewards, or perks.
You play for speed and accuracy, usually with a time limit or shot limit, and the targets can be customized.
